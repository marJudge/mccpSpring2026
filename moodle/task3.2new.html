Task 3.2: Writing Sample Collection for Gap Analysis

This task helps you improve your writing skills by collecting materials that will be used to identify gaps between your current writing and excellent academic writing, and to provide you with personalized feedback and recommendations.

What You Need to Do

Step 1: Understand the writing assignment requirements

Before collecting your writing samples, please read the writing assignment instructions to understand what you're working toward.

Step 2: Get the templates and instructions

Refer to the writingSampleCollection folder on GitHub for:

  • Complete instructions and requirements
  • Templates for your writing samples
  • Guidelines on what to include

Step 3: Set up your folder

  1. Pull the writingSampleCollection folder from the main repository to your private repository
  2. Create the folder at: PhDagentSpring2026/writing/writingSampleCollection/
  3. Copy the templates you need
  4. Fill in the templates with your content

Step 4: Collect your materials

You need to provide:

  1. Your writing sample (choose one):
    • First draft of Introduction and Literature Review (preferred), OR
    • Outline + 2-3 paragraph samples, OR
    • Previous writing that includes Introduction/Literature Review
  2. Comments/feedback you've received on your writing (supervisor, peer, reviewer, teacher comments - if available)
  3. Your reflection on writing challenges, process, AI usage, and goals

Step 5: Submit

  1. Update your files in the writingSampleCollection folder
  2. Sync to GitHub (commit and push)
  3. Reply to this Moodle post with a link to your folder in your private repository
  4. Include a brief note about what you've included

Important Information

Deadline: 15 February 2026

Purpose: This is a collection task - you're providing materials for analysis. The teacher will analyze your writing and provide personalized feedback. You don't need to do the analysis yourself.

What the teacher will do:

  • Read your writing sample and comments
  • Analyze gaps by comparing your writing with assessment rubrics and model papers
  • Consider your reflection and how you use AI
  • Provide a diagnostic report on gaps identified and recommendations for improvement

Access: The teacher already has edit access to your private repository (from Task 3.1), so you just need to provide the link to your folder.

Key Resources

  • Writing Instructions: Assignment requirements and rubrics
  • Templates and Instructions: writingSampleCollection folder

Submission Format

In your reply to this post, include:

Link to my folder:
https://github.com/[YourUsername]/[YourPrivateRepo]/tree/main/writing/writingSampleCollection

What I've included:
[Brief note: e.g., "First draft of Introduction and Literature Review, supervisor comments, and reflection on writing challenges"]

Questions? If you're unsure about what to include, refer to the README in the writingSampleCollection folder for detailed instructions and templates.

Remember: The goal is to help you improve. The more materials you provide, the better the feedback you'll receive. Even if you don't have a complete draft, an outline with sample paragraphs is acceptable.
